Six Schools. Six Countries. One Year.

Maker100 Leaders: Robotics, AI & IoT

Mission Looking for six pilot schools from six different countries. 3–30 students per school. Open-source curriculum. Approximately $200 USD per student in hardware. The goal is simple: gather evidence that AI and robotics education can scale globally.
